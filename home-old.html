    a programme to inspire entrepreneurial innovation

    We augment the school curriculum with an immersive learning ecosystem that cultivates the entrepreneurial mindset in students, encouraging them to explore their passions, think critically and creatively, learn essential problem-solving skills, and prepare for the challenges and opportunities of 21st-century careers.

    Coursebook
    A comprehensive syllabus covering Beginner, Intermediate and Advanced levels covered over three years, featuring the fundamentals of entrepreneurship including the SMART model, SWOT analysis, business plan concepts and more
    Download a preview chapter of the coursebook
    Masterclasses
    A series of monthly sessions by startup founders and industry experts for students, providing them insights, strategies, real-life anecdotes and an in-depth look into the practical applications of entrepreneurship concepts.
    All-India Quiz
    A national-level event that brings together young minds from diverse backgrounds to assess their understanding of entrepreneurial concepts and skills, encouraging creative and critical thinking.
    Mentorship
    A series of sessions led by business school faculty members and entrepreneurs, guiding students on crafting compelling business plans and delivering impactful pitches with clarity and precision.
    Launchpad
    A platform for students to showcase their ideas by presenting their business plans with a focus on value propositions, market opportunities, and growth strategies, to a panel of entrepreneurs and investors.
    Teacher Training
    A training session with reading and viewing material, and webinars/workshops, designed to assist teachers in conducting the classroom portion of the programme, equipping them with the knowledge and tools needed to integrate entrepreneurship education into the curriculum.

    a masterclass in entrepreneurial strategies

    We host online monthly masterclasses facilitated by startup founders and industry experts with a wealth of experience. The masterclasses are designed to equip students with essential entrepreneurial skills, focusing on identifying opportunities, developing ideas, and tackling business challenges.
    Students are given the opportunity to gain comprehensive insights through engaging activities and real-world examples, instilling confidence in their ability to adapt and innovate, fostering a culture of lifelong learning and exploration.

    a showcase of entrepreneurial excellence

    We offer students a platform to showcase their entrepreneurial ideas and competencies at a national level in three challenging stages, designed to foster collaboration, networking and presentation skills.
    Step 1: All-India Quiz
    A national-level quiz featuring challenging questions and scenarios designed to assess students in:
    • Understanding of entrepreneurial concepts and skills
    • Awareness of business strategies and market dynamics
    Step 2: Mentorship
    A round of online sessions by business school faculty and entrepreneurs to guide students in:
    • Identifying market opportunities
    • Developing business strategies
    • Honing their pitching skills
    • Assessing their business plans
    Step 3: Launchpad
    A national-level event offering a platform to students from diverse backgrounds to:
    • Showcase business plans entrepreneurs or investors
    • Acquire valuable insights and constructive feedback

    a profile of entrepreneurial vision

    Meet Karan, a student participating in an entrepreneurship skills programme at his school. Karan is fascinated by history and he loves talking about the intriguing historical figures and events that have shaped the world. His peers appreciate his style of articulation which leads him to wonder if he can reach out to a larger audience through social media and monetize his content.
    Here's what he does to turn his passion into a sustainable income:
    Market research
    Karan speaks to his friends and gauges their interest in learning about history through short-form videos.
    Business plan
    Karan formulates a strategy to start an Instagram page and YouTube channel to feature his videos every week.
    Marketing strategy
    Karan explores branding and marketing strategies to understand the best fit to reach his target audience.
    Investor pitching
    Karan offers investors an opportunity to sponsor his channel thereby reaching out to the teenage demographic.
    *The character of Karan is a fictional example created solely for illustrative purposes.
